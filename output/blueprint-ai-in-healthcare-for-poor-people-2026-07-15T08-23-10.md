#### Primary Revenue Streams
To address the financial constraints of our target audience while ensuring the viability of our operations, we propose the following primary revenue streams:

* **Subscription-based Service (SaaS)**: Offer AI-powered health diagnostic tools and personalized health plans through a tiered subscription model. Pricing will be based on the level of access to premium features, frequency of consultations, and depth of data analysis.
	+ **Freemium Tier**: Basic access to AI-driven symptom checkers and limited health insights at no cost.
	+ **Premium Tier** ($19/month): Enhanced AI-driven diagnostics, personalized nutrition advice, and quarterly consultation sessions with certified health professionals.
	+ **Pro Tier** ($49/month): Comprehensive health monitoring, including continuous glucose tracking, mental health support, and bi-monthly consultations.
* **Partnerships with NGOs and Government Agencies**: Collaborate with non-governmental organizations (NGOs) and government bodies focused on global health initiatives. These partnerships could provide funding or subsidies for our services, aiming to increase healthcare accessibility among disadvantaged communities.

#### Secondary Revenue Streams
In addition to primary revenue sources, the following secondary streams can contribute to the overall revenue:

* **Data Analytics Services**: Anonymized and aggregated health data insights sold to pharmaceutical companies, research institutions, and healthcare providers. This would require strict adherence to privacy regulations and ethical standards.
* **Advertising and Sponsored Content**: Relevant, non-intrusive advertising from reputable health-related businesses within our platform, ensuring alignment with our values and the protection of user privacy.

#### Unit Economics
Our unit economics will focus on achieving the following targets:
